MAPQUEST ALERT!!
Due to the fact that the Post Office which servs the Big Buck is miles away, MAPQUEST may not be accurate! Do this: enter your address as “from” and Cross Anchor, SC as “destination”- then  add “Hwy. 49N 3miles to Big Buck on left.” 

From Charlotte: I-77 to Hwy. 322 exit in Rock Hill. Follow Hwy. 322 to Hwy. 49South to Lockhart. Follow Hwy. 49South through Union and Cross Keys to Big Buck Racing on right.

Or: I-77 to exit 65 to Chester. Follow Hwy. 49South from Chester through Lockhart, Union, and Cross Keys to Big Buck Racing on right.

Or. I-85 to Spartanburg.  I-26 towards Columbia to exit 44. Turn left on Hwy 49 North through Cross Anchor to Big Buck Racing on left.

From Greenville: I-385 to exit 5. Turn left on Hwy. 49North through Cross Anchor to Big Buck Racing on left.

From Spartanburg: I-26 towards Columbia to exit 44. Turn left on Hwy. 49North through Cross Anchor to Big Buck Racing on left.

Or: Hwy. 176 into Union. At 5th. Traffic light turn right on Hwy. 49South through Cross Keys to Big Buck Racing on right.

From Columbia: I-26 towards Spartanburg to exit 44. Turn right on Hwy. 49North through Cross Anchor to Big Buck Racing on left.

Or: I-26 towards Spartanburg to Hwy. 56 exit. Turn right on Hwy.56 to Cross Anchor. Turn right on Hwy. 49North to Big Buck Racing on left.

From Atlanta: I-85North to Greenville. I-385South to exit 5. Turn left on Hwy.49North through Cross Anchor to Big Buck Racing on left.

From Big Buck Racing to Union: Go through gate, turn left onto Hwy.49North 13 miles to Union. This is closest access to Wal- Mart, Auto Parts, Building Supply House, Restaurants, Radio Shack, Grocery Store, Ace Hardware, Hospital, etc.
